INTRODUCTION
After the collapse of civilization or a major disaster, the disruption of the food supply chain will become the most severe survival challenge. Mastering basic agricultural skills is not only about personal survival but also the cornerstone for rebuilding human civilization. This guide focuses on low-tech, high-efficiency planting methods that can be implemented even in situations with scarce resources and limited tools. Choosing the right crops is crucial - what we need are nutrient-dense, easy-to-grow varieties that can be self-propagated. This guide will center on potatoes, beans, and leafy vegetables, as these three types of crops can provide the basic combination of carbohydrates, proteins, and vitamins.
